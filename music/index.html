I love to sing and perform

I have been singing since I was 5 years old.

I have sung in choirs, acapella groups, theater performances, and—finally at Kellogg—a rock band!

I managed to continue my passion for musical theater beyond college, and participated in 1-2 musical productions a year in the Bay Area. Some of my favorites shows include:

A New Brain, Director and Actor (Mimi Schwinn) Sing Club 2014
Carrie The Musical (Ensemble) Ray of Light Theater 2013
The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee (Olive Ostrovsky) Sing Club 2012
The Full Monty (Dance Instructor, Dolores Ray of Light Theater 2012
Company (Sarah, Marta, Larry) Sing Club 2011
Jerry Springer the Opera (Ensemble) Ray of Light Theater 2010
